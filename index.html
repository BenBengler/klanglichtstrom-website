Klang-/Licht STROM

“Klang-/Licht STROM” is a collaborative performance project of Ben Bengler (music, live electronics) and Thomas Geissl (light, programming). It’s driven by our shared vision for creating performances where the audience is in the centre of things, with sound and light unfolding around them.

Largely free from strictly timed sequencing, I create and evolve the music via real-time audio manipulation and layered sound processing techniques. The sound is analysed in real-time and Geissl uses the derived data as a raw material, augmenting and manipulating it with a custom software tool to creatively drive the the spatial light setup.

In this way, we try to conjure up an interconnected and fluid choreography of sound and light unfolding around the audience.
